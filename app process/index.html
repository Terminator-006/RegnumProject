Application Process

Welcome to Regnum

Regnum is an exclusive community for elite couples seeking extraordinary experiences and meaningful connections. Our application process is designed to ensure that all members meet our high standards of excellence and exclusivity. Below, you will find a detailed overview of the steps involved in applying for membership.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Initial Interest and Waitlist

  • Join the Waitlist: Begin by entering your email address on our waitlist page to express your interest in joining Regnum. This will ensure you receive updates and notifications about the application process.

2. Application Form

  • Personal Information: Provide your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and country of residence.
  • Financial Details: Disclose your annual income and list any luxury assets you own, such as yachts, private jets, or high-end cars.
  • Educational Background: Share details about your highest degree obtained and the institution(s) you graduated from.
  • Social and Cultural Engagement: List any exclusive clubs or organizations you are a member of.
  • Personal Preferences: Specify your preferences for other couples, including qualities, activities, age range, locations, frequency of interaction, cultural interests, hobbies, dining preferences, travel preferences, lifestyle, health and fitness interests, communication preferences, social media presence, pet preferences, event participation, and personal values.
  • Travel Information: Describe the most exotic place you have traveled to and provide your visa number issued for international travel.
  • References or Endorsements: Provide names and contact information for any references or endorsements (optional).
  • Motivation: Explain why you want to join Regnum and what you hope to gain from being a member.

3. Submission and Review

  • Submit Your Application: Once you have completed the application form, submit it for review. You will receive a confirmation email acknowledging receipt of your application.
  • Application Review: Our team will carefully review your application to ensure it meets our criteria for membership. This may include verifying the information provided and conducting background checks if necessary.

4. Approval and Membership Fee

  • Membership Approval: If your application is approved, you will receive an email notifying you of your acceptance into the Regnum community.
  • Payment of Membership Fee: Upon approval, you will be required to pay a membership fee to access the full benefits of Regnum. The fee amount and payment instructions will be included in your approval email.

5. Access to Member Benefits

  • Welcome to Regnum: Once your membership fee is processed, you will receive a welcome package, including your membership number and virtual card.
  • Member Zone Access: Gain access to our exclusive member zone, where you can explore detailed profiles of other distinguished couples, participate in exclusive events, and engage in extraordinary adventures together.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the eligibility criteria for joining Regnum?

Regnum is an exclusive community for elite couples. Applicants must meet our criteria for financial status, educational background, social engagement, and personal preferences.

How long does the application review process take?

The application review process typically takes 7-10 business days. You will be notified via email once a decision has been made.

What happens if my application is not approved?

If your application is not approved, you will receive an email notification with feedback. You may reapply after addressing any areas that did not meet our criteria.

Is the membership fee refundable?

Membership fees are generally non-refundable. Please refer to ourRefund & Cancellation Policy for more details.
