Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I apply for membership at Regnum?

Visit our Application Process page for detailed instructions on how to apply for membership.

What are the eligibility criteria for joining Regnum?

Regnum is an exclusive community for elite couples. Applicants must meet our criteria for financial status, educational background, social engagement, and personal preferences. Detailed criteria can be found on our Application Process page.

How can I check the status of my application?

You will receive email updates regarding the status of your application. If you have any questions about your application status, please contact us at concierge@regnum.life.

What if I need to update my application information?

If you need to update any information on your application, please email us at concierge@regnum.life with the details you need to change. Our team will assist you in making the necessary updates.

How do I cancel my membership?

To cancel your membership, please contact our support team at concierge@regnum.life. Your cancellation will be effective at the end of your current billing cycle. Please refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy for more details.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your privacy is important to us. We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details on how we handle your data.
